Scrambler Eggs


Eggs are one of the favorite foods.Here is a recipe for decliously rich scrambled eggs.


Ingredients


  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method


  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted and cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate untill seved)
